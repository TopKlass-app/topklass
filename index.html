Privacy Policy

TopKlass! does not collect any personal data.

TopKlass! is a school agenda application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

2. Data We Collect

TopKlass! does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (tasks, grades, schedule) is stored exclusively on your device.
